    When choosing a clarinet, one must be aware of the elements composing its structure, meaning a clarinet made out of wood may sound or play differently than one made out of plastic. There are also different accessories that come with different brands of clarinet, some of which are more favorable to others. For example, some brands come with sufficient cleaning mechanisms such as swabs or cork grease, but some brands carry none of them. When choosing a clarinet, make sure you are getting your actual money's worth!
